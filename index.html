RideMeet is a modern platform designed for motorcycle enthusiasts to effortlessly organize meetups, create travel plans, and collaborate within riding organizations. Whether you're hosting local meetups or planning a multi-day road trip, RideMeet helps you keep everything in sync — from dates and participants to gear and hotel bookings.

What Can You Do With RideMeet?

Whether you're planning a quick local ride or a cross-country adventure, RideMeet gives you the tools to coordinate every detail. Built with riders in mind, every feature is designed to make planning simple, social, and stress-free. Here’s what RideMeet can do for you:

Effortless Ride Meetups

Create, join, and manage motorcycle meetups with ease. Whether it's a casual weekend ride or a large community event, RideMeet lets you:


  • Schedule meetups with precise dates
  • Control who can attend (open or by invitation)
  • Track RSVP status of each rider
    Pair this with location pins and time slots — and you're good to go.

Structure Through Riding Organizations

Form or join motorcycle organizations to ride with your crew. Within each organization, you can:


  • Manage members and assign roles
  • Create meetups on behalf of the organization
  • Restrict events to specific members A single rider can be part of multiple groups — ride with friends, clubs, or national teams.

Advanced Trip Planning (Coming Soon)

Trips are multi-day ride events with deep planning capabilities. Perfect for cross-country tours, these allow you to:


  • Plan each day of the ride with routes and stops
  • See who's riding and what they’re bringing
  • Organize sleeping arrangements (who sleeps with whom)
  • Add hotels, ferry tickets, tolls — and split the costs automatically
  • Add tasks and responsibilities (who brings tools, gear, food)
  • Track everyone's location and nearby mechanics RideMeet turns chaos into clarity for your next big adventure.
